Arcade

The Arcade is the home for the various games Ratel offers. Here you convert your Ratel Points into Chips and play with them.

/arcade

When you first open the page you'll see a wallet card up top. The balance starts at 0 chips. Below it is a row of game cards. There's only one game in v1 — Fact or Fold — and new games will be added to the same row over time.

What a session looks like​

Every game in the Arcade follows the same rhythm.

  1. Top up your chip wallet. Convert some RP into chips. You only need to do this again when the balance runs low.
  2. Pick a game. Click whichever card catches your eye on the arcade landing page.
  3. Hit Play and the game begins. For multiplayer games you'll be sent to a lobby to wait for the other players.
  4. Play the game. Just follow the stages the game walks you through.
  5. The settle screen shows the result. Your chip balance moves and the same screen asks whether you'd like to register the writing you produced into your Essence. You decide what to keep.

You can do this once a day or three times a day. A casual player who runs a few rounds a week will see their balance grow round by round.

Your chip wallet​

The Arcade plays in chips rather than RP directly. Think of it the way you'd think about converting cash into chips at a casino. You convert once and the chips stay in your account without expiring. The wallet lives at:

/arcade/wallet

A few things worth knowing.

  • The smallest conversion is 100 RP. Small enough to top up right before a session without thinking about it.
  • Chip-to-RP conversion isn't open yet. Chips you win stay in your balance for the next round.
  • The buy-in locks the moment you hit Play. Not when the round actually starts — when you queue up. If you change your mind in a lobby that hasn't started yet, just hit Leave. The chips come right back.
tip

You don't have to think about chips while a round is running. Top up the wallet once and play whenever you feel like it. The settle screen credits you back automatically — no separate claim button to press.
