Framing the conversation

Carbon offsets remain one of the most misunderstood mechanisms in climate policy. In this thread we want to collect practical, verifiable strategies that city governments can deploy in the next 18 months.

Before diving in, please read the three references linked in the attachments section and bring at least one concrete example from your region.

Discussion questions

  • Which verification standards (Gold Standard, Verra, Puro) have you actually audited in practice?
  • How do you reconcile offset purchases with direct-reduction targets?
  • What does a credible community-level offset program look like?

Keep it practical. Anecdotes with numbers over theory.
