Q2 Governance Proposal: Treasury Diversification Strategy

Alice Park @alice.eth
Apr 8, 2026 Governance

This proposal outlines a strategy for diversifying our DAO treasury to reduce single-asset risk and ensure sustainable funding for operations through Q3 2026.

Background

Currently, 87% of our treasury is held in a single governance token. While this demonstrates strong alignment, it exposes us to significant volatility risk that could impact our ability to fund ongoing development.

"A resilient treasury is the foundation of a resilient organization." — DeFi Treasury Management Best Practices

Proposed Allocation

  • 50% — Governance Token (maintain voting power)
  • 25% — Stablecoins (USDC/DAI) for operational expenses
  • 15% — ETH for gas and ecosystem participation
  • 10% — DeFi yield strategies (low-risk only)

Implementation Timeline

The diversification would be executed over a 6-week period using TWAP orders to minimize market impact. Each phase would be approved by a multi-sig vote before execution.

We welcome all community members to share their thoughts on this proposal. Your input is valuable in shaping the direction of our treasury management strategy.
